## üîß How to Update Sitemap

When you add new pages, update `public/sitemap.xml`:

```xml
<url>
  <loc>https://www.printerzsupport.com/new-page</loc>
  <lastmod>2024-12-18</lastmod>
  <changefreq>monthly</changefreq>
  <priority>0.8</priority>
</url>
```
